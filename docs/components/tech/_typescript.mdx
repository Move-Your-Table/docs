Typescript turns Javascript, which is a weak typed language, into a strong type language when developing and compiles it to Javascript.
This gives us better tooling while developing and reduces errors.

Its factor as a trending topic can also not be ignored: In 2020, it was the 2nd most loved programming language & was used by 78% of all developers, with 93% saying that they would use it again, according to the Stackoverflow Survey of 2020 & State of Javascript of 2020.
